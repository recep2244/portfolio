About Me

I am a dedicated Bioinformatician with a unique academic background spanning Physics and Bioengineering. Currently serving as a Research Associate at the University of Reading, I focus on the improvement of protein tertiary and quaternary structure predictions.

My work involves developing and refining advanced tools like the IntFOLD server, utilizing Molecular Dynamics simulations, and applying Machine Learning techniques to solve complex biological problems. I am passionate about bridging the gap between computational physics and molecular biology to unravel the mysteries of protein structures.
